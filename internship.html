PFE INTERNSHIP 2025 (4 months)

Comparative Study of Large Language Models (LLM)

**Anavid Services**, Sfax, Tunisia

17th Febrery 2025 - 17th May 2025

Objective & Principal Missions:

  • This internship aims to develop an intelligent chatbot capable of extracting, structuring, and delivering information from the company's internal data.
  • The project will begin with a comparative analysis of open-source LLM models to identify the one that best meets the chatbot's specific needs.
  • Next, fine-tuning will be performed on a set of internal data, supplemented by a synthetic database to enhance the relevance of the responses provided.
  • Finally, the chatbot will be integrated into a functional environment and optimized to ensure high performance.

Keywords:

  • Python - PyTorch - TensorFlow - Hugging Face Transformers
